Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Health Tracker ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application is a health and lifestyle utility designed to help users track water consumption, calculate Body Mass Index (BMI), log weight progress, and view daily health tips.

For terms relating to usage constraints and medical disclaimers, please read our Terms of Use.

1. Information Collection and Use

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The Application collects user data to provide and improve its services. This data is split between local storage on your device and secure cloud storage:

A. Cloud Storage (Firebase Firestore)

To enable account registration, profile recovery, and interactive questionnaire submissions, we store the following parameters securely on Firebase servers:

  • Authentication Info: Your email address and login credentials (managed securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • User Profile Parameters: Full Name, Age, Height, Current Weight, Target Weight, WhatsApp Number, and City.
  • Questionnaire Responses: Answers submitted to assessments.

B. Local Storage (SharedPreferences)

All fitness logging data, specifically:

  • Daily water intake logging progress.
  • Weight logs and progress updates.

are saved locally on your device for fast access and to minimize internet consumption. This local data is not shared with third parties.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application utilizes third-party services that collect data to facilitate core functionalities. These services include:

  • Google Play Services: Used for system-level API connections.
  • Firebase Authentication: Used to authenticate users securely.
  • Google Firebase Firestore: Used to save user profiles and questionnaires.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to log crash data and errors.

3. User Account and Data Deletion

In compliance with Google Play Store developer policies, users have full control over their data:

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the application by navigating to the Profile Screen and tapping Delete Account.
  • Confirming this action will immediately and permanently delete your user profile document from Firestore, remove your authentication credentials from Firebase, and clear all local logging caches.

4. Data Security

We take security seriously. All data transmitted between the Application and Firebase is encrypted in transit using industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) / Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocols. Cloud databases are protected by strict Firebase Security Rules that limit data access solely to the authenticated owner of the profile.

5. Children's Privacy

This Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete this from our servers immediately.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@healthtrackapp.com.
